Steve Does PowerShell 37:02

• Making PowerShell Modules
• Windows PowerShell Location:
• User: "C:\Users\<username>\Documents\WindowsPowerShell\Modules"
• System: "C:\Program Files\WindowsPowerShell\Modules"
• PowerShell Core (6 and higher) Location:
• User: 'C:\Users\<username>\Documents\PowerShell\Modules'
• System: 'C:\Program Files\PowerShell\7-preview\Modules'
• Macs:
• User: "~/.local/share/powershell/Modules"
• System: "/usr/local/share/powershell/Modules"
